Who I Am

Throughout my course of growing up I had a few times switched schools. Though not the worst thing imaginable, going from private to public was something that I found to be somewhat difficult. It took some getting used to, to say the least.

Making friends and then leaving those friends behind, time and time again, proved to be somewhat difficult for me. I had throughout the course of my childhood switched school districts three times-- each time seemingly "at the good part" -- being when I built friendships that I valued.
